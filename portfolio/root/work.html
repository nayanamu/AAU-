Craigslist Mobile App Design

Craigslist mobile app was a group project of 3 people. The idea was to design a Mobile app for Craigslist which is clutter free, clean, intuitive and simple interface.

Role:

I was involved in research, conceptual design, layout, and prototyping.

Challenges:

Over the years Craigslist has become a very popular website among people for classified advertisements. It is a platform to get better and cheaper stuff, but it still has no official mobile app. The website seems bit cluttered and extremely unorganized which sometimes makes it difficult for users to use it easily.

Solution:

A well designed mobile app which can help users to perform their tasks easily and efficiently. Provides organized categories to users and to incorporate everything from the website to the app. To provide the user with intuitive navigations with notifications and alerts.

Personal Goals:

  • Make navigation as simple and intuitive as possible.
  • Give a branding to the Craigslist.
  • Make users want to return the app.

Tools

  • Sketch

On the basis of preliminary workflow and wireframes I made new design. I made few visual improvement and introduced new feature "Set Alert" for the users.

Version 1

Personal Goals:

After I made wireframe version 3 I did some more research, user testing and learned more about UX principles and Material design which I applied in the final design. Few takeaways from testing and research:

  • Make the style consistent and keep the design simple with clear visible elements.
  • Make interactions delightful and to communicate.
  • Simplify Post Ad process and separate "Set Alert" feature from profile page
